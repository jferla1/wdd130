My Favorite City

My favorite city is Mexicali, Baja California, Mexico. It is known as the “City that Captured the Sun” because of its warm desert climate, with bright sunshine almost all year long. Mexicali is a border city next to Calexico, California,and is famous for its unique blend of Mexican and Chinese culture, especially its renowned Mexicali-style Chinese food. The city is also an important center for industry, agriculture, and education in northern Mexico, while still offering a relaxed, friendly atmosphere that makes it special to both locals and visitors.
